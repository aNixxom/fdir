About Us

Our agency was founded on the belife that every american has a right to his or hers data online. The main goal of our agency is to stop the tech compainies like Google, Facebook, Twitter, Apple, and so much more from stealing and using your data against you. We aim to do this task starting by passing the 21st Century Regulation of Information Technology Act. Gettings this bill passed into law will allow the FDIR to take actions against the companies who use the everyday american for their own financial gains. One way we will achive this is by require all tech compainies to submit a copy of their TOS (Terms of Server) to the FDIR (Federal Department of Internet Regulation) in order for us to review them. If we find anything out of place or unfair for the user it will then be our mission to find the people responsibe and give them what they desever, weather it be jail time, or a fine of up to 200% of that compainies annual incom. We will also aim to provie a safer and less censored enviromenet of the people of the United States. As well as require companies to follow more internet regulation guidlines.
